1. Brand Overview

What is Juncta?

Juncta is an AI project management tool that takes on the cognitive work of planning, organising, and managing — so users can focus on judgment, relationships, and decisions. The name comes from Latin jungere — to join, to connect.

Brand Personality

Juncta's brand is precise, understated, and trustworthy. The visual language draws from architectural and engineering aesthetics — clean grids, deliberate spacing, monospace labels. It avoids flashy gradients, bold colours, and tech-startup clichés. The accent colour (burnt orange) is used sparingly for emphasis, not decoration.

Tagline

AI proposes. Humans decide.

Secondary: "You don't manage projects in Juncta. You manage them with it."

2. Logo

The Juncta logo is an interlocking block mark — two rectangular forms joined by a notch. It represents connection, structure, and the interlock between human judgment and AI capability.

Clear Space & Minimum Size

Maintain clear space equal to the height of one logo block on all sides. Minimum size: 20px height for digital, 8mm for print.

✓ DO

Use on solid backgrounds (light, dark, or cream). Pair with Geist Mono wordmark. Maintain aspect ratio.

✗ DON'T

Place on busy backgrounds. Stretch, rotate, or add effects. Change the notch proportions. Use colours other than accent, stone, or white.

7. Motion & Animation

Easing Curves

NameValueUsage
Expo outcubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)Scroll reveals, transitions
Springcubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)Button hover, interactive elements

Scroll Reveal

Elements fade in from 24px below with 0.8s duration on intersection. Staggered delays available: 0.1s, 0.2s, 0.3s, 0.4s. The reveal style is subtle and professional — no bouncing, sliding, or dramatic entrances.

Navbar

The navbar uses a frosted glass effect: backdrop-filter: blur(20px) saturate(1.8). Background shifts from 72% to 92% opacity on scroll, with a border appearing at 20px scroll depth.

8. Tone of Voice

Writing Principles

PrincipleDescription
PreciseSay what we mean. Avoid vague claims. "Juncta walks the dependency graph" not "Juncta helps with dependencies."
Confident, not boastfulState capabilities directly. No superlatives. No "revolutionary" or "game-changing."
Conversational authorityWrite as a knowledgeable peer, not a sales deck. Short sentences. Active voice.
Human-centredThe human makes the decisions. Juncta proposes, organises, computes. Never "Juncta decides."

✓ DO

"Not notifications. Computation."

"You bring domain knowledge. Juncta brings methodology and memory."

"An experienced peer who works for you."

✗ DON'T

"The most powerful AI project management platform ever built."

"Juncta revolutionizes how teams collaborate."

"Let AI take over your project management."
